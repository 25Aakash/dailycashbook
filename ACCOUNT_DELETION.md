# Account Deletion Request - DailyCashBook

To request deletion of your DailyCashBook account and associated data, please follow these steps:

## How to Request Account Deletion

1. Send an email to: **dailycashbook3@gmail.com**
2. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**
3. Include the following information in your email:
   - Your registered mobile number
   - Your registered email address
   - Reason for deletion (optional)

## What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

- ✅ Your account information (name, email, mobile number)
- ✅ All expense and income transactions
- ✅ Custom categories you created
- ✅ User preferences and settings
- ✅ Authentication credentials

## Data Retention Period

- Your account and all associated data will be **deleted within 90 days** of your request
- During this 90-day period, you can contact us to cancel the deletion request
- After 90 days, the deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

## Backup Your Data (Optional)

Before requesting deletion, you may want to:
- Export your transaction data from the Reports section
- Take screenshots of important records
- Save any information you might need later

## Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact us at:
**dailycashbook3@gmail.com**
